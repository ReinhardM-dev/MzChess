Game Editor

The game editor is based on Qt’s QTreeWidget.

It has 2 types of lines:

  • regular moves where all columns have a white background

  • beginning of a variant where the middle columns have a grey background

The tree widget has 4 colums:

  1. Move shows the actual move or the beginning of a variant in SAN notation

  2. Ann shows the annotation, i.e. a symbolic move assessment

  3. Pos shows the position assessment

  4. Score shows the engine or material score of the last move in pawns, material score ending with M

  5. Comment shows either the move comment or starting comment of a variant

By clicking the annotation (Ann) and position assessment (Pos) fields, a popup dialog opens which allows to change the contents
